About Stax™ Aviation Group

Stax™ Aviation Group is a family-owned company built by aviators, technologists, and problem-solvers who know hangar operations from the inside out.

Our story began with a simple mission: bring clarity and efficiency to one of the industry’s most overlooked challenges—aircraft stacking and hangar management. Our founder and CTO, a Vietnam-era veteran and former Apple management engineer, saw firsthand how smart tools could transform complex systems. So he brought that thinking to aviation.

Today, Stax is the first and only software platform built specifically for FBOs, MROs, operators, and airport authorities to visualize, plan, and manage their space—safely and profitably. Our technology blends precision with real-world practicality, and our family-run approach means we’re hands-on, responsive, and deeply invested in every customer’s success.

Trusted to manage 2,000+ hangars and ramps worldwide, Stax is modernizing hangar management—one stack at a time.
